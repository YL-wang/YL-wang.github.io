I am a Postdoctoral Associate in the School of Artificial Intelligence, Jilin University, selected as a Dingxin Scholar, supervised by Prof. Yi Chang. Previously, I participated in a CSC joint training program at Griffith University from November 2023 to November 2024, where I worked with Prof. Shirui Pan on out-of-distribution graph detection and graph prompt learning. I received my Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence from Jilin University, under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Xin Wang.
My research focuses on: (1) Graph Representation Learning; (2) Graph Out-of-Distribution Detection/Generalization; (3) LLMs for Graph Learning; and (4) Graph for Multi Agents. I aim to develop scalable, robust, and trustworthy AI algorithms for complex graph-structured data, with applications spanning scientific discovery and intelligent systems. My work has been published in leading journals and conferences, including TPAMI, KDD, ACL, ICLR, ICML, AAAI, EMNLP, IJCAI, and CIKM.
