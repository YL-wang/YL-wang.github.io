Selected Research

Graph Learning; LLMs for Graphs; Multi-Agent Systems; AI for Science

Graph Learning & Robustness

My research studies scalable, robust, and generalizable graph learning algorithms, with a focus on graph representation learning, graph OOD generalization, graph anomaly detection, and graph robustness.

LLMs for Graphs & Multi-Agent Systems

I am interested in integrating large language models with graph learning and multi-agent systems, especially for trustworthy reasoning, graph anomaly detection, and communication robustness.
